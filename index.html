TL;DR

Awake is being built for reliable mornings and focused days.

Set alarms that require action, build routines around habits, run Pomodoro-style focus sessions, track progress, and plan across time zones. Awake is currently in development for iPhone, with launch updates available through the waitlist.

Features

Everything planned for waking up, focusing, and following through.

01

Mission alarms

Dismiss alarms with math, memory, movement, QR or barcode scans, and other tasks that make it harder to fall back asleep.

02

Accountability shield

Stronger wake-up controls for serious users, including snooze limits, volume guidance, and safety checks around alarm dismissal.

03

Focus timer

Pomodoro sessions, ambient focus sounds, strict mode ideas, and session history designed for deep work and study blocks.

04

Plans and habits

Daily tasks, habit streaks, routines, and future habit alarms that can tie real accountability to the things you want to repeat.

05

Global time planner

Compare time zones, find meeting overlap, and plan across cities for remote work, freelancers, teams, and families.

06

Progress analytics

Track wake-up consistency, snooze patterns, focus sessions, routine completion, and habit progress over time.

Why it exists

Most alarm apps ring. Awake is designed to make you respond.

For heavy sleepers

Use high-friction missions so stopping the alarm requires proof.

For distracted mornings

Turn wake-up into a routine with tasks, habit checks, and timers.

For focused work

Move from waking up to getting work done with time-boxed sessions.

FAQ

Common questions before launch.

Is Awake available now?

Awake is currently coming soon for iOS. Join the waitlist to get launch and beta availability updates.

Will Awake be available on Android?

The first release is focused on iPhone. Android support may be considered later based on demand.

What makes Awake different from a normal alarm?

Awake is designed around accountability. Missions, routines, focus sessions, habits, and progress history are planned together instead of treating the alarm as a standalone clock.
