Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Awake is currently in development. This policy explains the expected privacy approach for the website and upcoming iOS app. It may be updated before launch.

Information we collect

If you contact us or join the waitlist, we may receive your name, email address, message, and stated feature interest. The website does not currently include account creation or payment processing.

How information is used

We use submitted information to respond to messages, manage waitlist interest, send launch updates, understand requested features, and improve the product roadmap.

iOS app data

Awake may use alarms, notifications, motion, camera, health-related, timer, blocked app selection, and Screen Time-related data where users grant permission. Final app data handling will be described clearly before public launch.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact hello@awakeapp.net.
